Architecture

Five services. One pipeline.

Each service does exactly one thing. Together they take a raw sensor reading to a dispatched mission in under 2 seconds.

01
Fusion :8002
Multi-sensor track fusion
Kalman EKF across camera, ADS-B, AIS, MAVLink, CoT/ATAK. M-of-N confirmation. Cross-camera re-identification.
02
Intelligence :8003
Risk scoring + auto-dispatch
ONNX risk scorer classifies severity. ONNX mission classifier selects mission type. Direct REST call to Tasking — no LLM in the critical path.
03
Tasking :8004
Mission lifecycle + terrain
State machine: PENDING → ACTIVE → COMPLETE. SRTM DEM terrain following. OPA policy enforcement. Full replay timeline.
04
Fabric :8001
World state + alerting
Live entity stream over WebSocket. MQTT bridge. Alert escalation with configurable timeout. CRDT mesh replication.
05
Console :3002
Operator interface
MapLibre live map. Alert queue with investigate flow. Live HLS video overlay. Mission replay scrubber. Three role views: OPS, COMMAND, DEV.

Capabilities

Production-grade, out of the box

Everything a civilian autonomous operations platform needs. No assembly required.

⚡
Autonomous mission dispatch
Trained ML model (GradientBoosting, ONNX, <1ms) decides mission type and dispatches immediately. 108k real-world training observations. No LLM required.
🔀
Multi-sensor fusion
Kalman EKF track fusion across camera, ADS-B, AIS, MAVLink, CoT/ATAK, and any custom sensor. M-of-N confirmation. Cross-camera re-identification.
🧠
Self-improving AI
Retrain the mission planner on your own operator decisions with one command. The more you use it, the better it gets at your specific deployment context.
🗺️
Terrain awareness
SRTM DEM integration. All mission waypoints automatically adjusted to maintain true AGL altitude over real terrain. Elevation profiles for line-of-sight analysis.
📹
Live video
HLS streaming from any RTSP source — IP cameras, drone gimbal feeds. Sub-5-second latency. Playable in any browser, no plugins.
⏪
Mission replay
Every mission recorded as a time-indexed snapshot stream. Operators can scrub back through any incident for debrief or training.
📡
CoT / ATAK compatible
Bidirectional UDP bridge. Entities visible to any ATAK device on the network. Works with existing tactical comms infrastructure.
🔔
Alert escalation
Unacknowledged alerts auto-escalate via webhook + email. Configurable timeout per severity level. Full audit trail.
🔌
30-minute hardware integration
One base class, two methods. Subclass SummitAdapter and your hardware is live. Built-in adapters for DJI/MAVLink, ONVIF, OpenSky ADS-B, AIS vessels.
AI Coverage

Seven mission types.
108,000 training observations.

Trained on NASA FIRMS fire data, NOAA Storm Events, GBIF wildlife observations, and 20k synthetic examples across wildfire, flood, SAR, maritime, hazmat, agricultural, industrial, and more.

SURVEY
Fire, smoke, flood, earthquake damage, crop anomaly → UAV or fixed-wing survey pass
MONITOR
Person, survivor, vessel, vehicle, wildlife → UAV loiter overhead
SEARCH
Missing person, distress signal, mayday, overdue vessel → UAV grid pattern
PERIMETER
Hazmat spill, tornado, storm surge, intrusion, armed threat → UAV boundary patrol
ORBIT
Suspicious drone, vessel tracking, persistent ISR → UAV continuous orbit
DELIVER
Aid drop, medical supply, cargo, resupply → UAV delivery waypoint
INSPECT
Pipeline, power line, bridge, solar farm, wind turbine → UAV close-pass inspection
